Disability is Human

Almost everyone will temporarily or permanently experience disability at some point in their life. An estimated 1.3 billion people – about 16% of the global population – currently experience significant disability. This figure is rising as the population ages and noncommunicable diseases become more common.

Our Duty Towards Society

Some disabilities are visible, such as physical disabilities, and some are hidden; including mental health conditions, medical conditions, learning and cognitive disabilities.

We came together to raise awareness of a crucial gap in equity and create a more accessible tool in effort of full inclusion of disabilities within the internet. Through providing a variety of resources, we hope to assist disabled people navigate life and perform to their full potential.
